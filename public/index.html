ABOUT WELP

OUR AIM

With this project we wanted to create a platform that can help people in need of medical help, also this is a platform on which people can upload medical information for others.

OUR MOTIVE

Our motive is "WE TOGETHER CAN HELP" with this moto we created WELP. Our motivation came from the people who tried to share the information related to the medical facilities during the pendamic on their social accounts but that information was only limited to a very small crowd. So, we wanted to increase the reach for both, the one who requires help and the one who can help to a larger scale based on the location.
